About

Hello, I am Benoît Chédru (alias. B.C. Mix Music). I am a 19 year old French/Korean composer, producer, and artist that produces music on flat.io and Logic Pro X. I have been making music for approximately 5 years now and I have been a pianist for the past 11 years.

I composed approximately 70 songs so far, and my biggest achievements were the first release of my EP: I'm also human, last february, and several composing competitions that I have participated in (Piano Battles). I have also collaborated with other artists through a composer network I have created, called MCS (ie. Music Composing Station)
